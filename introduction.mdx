**Velobase is an open-source, high-performance billing ledger designed for AI applications.**

When you build AI apps, billing is hard because you don't know the exact cost upfront (e.g., streaming LLM output). Velobase solves this by acting as a high-speed "credit bank" for your application.

## Core Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Freeze-Consume Model" icon="layer-group">
    Safely freeze an estimated amount of credits before an AI generation, and consume the exact amount afterward. Zero risk of negative balances.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="High Concurrency" icon="bolt">
    Built to handle thousands of token deductions per second without race conditions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="API First" icon="code">
    Manage customers, top up balances, and deduct credits purely via REST API.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
